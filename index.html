HOW IT WORKS

The system.
No jargon.

Not a habit tracker. Not a to-do list. An AI-powered execution system built for ambitious 15–22 year olds who have goals but keep failing to follow through.

01
ANSWER 17 QUESTIONS
About your goals, past attempts, habits, and patterns. Not a personality quiz — diagnostic inputs the same ones a performance coach uses. Then 3 mission-specific questions.
02
AI RUNS 5 ANALYSIS LAYERS
Five AI calls score your consistency, path confusion, confidence, pressure, and trajectory gap. Every answer used. Combined = your Execution DNA profile.
03
GET YOUR PERSONAL ROADMAP
Phase 0: a 7-day plan built around your exact failure pattern. Not generic advice. Your system fix. Daily tasks calibrated to how many hours you actually have each day.
04
SUBMIT PROOF. OR RESET.
No proof = no credit. Miss one day = streak resets to zero. All 7 days verified = consistency score recalculated from real behaviour. Phase 1 unlocks.
// 3 WAYS TO PROVE YOUR WORK
💬
TEXT PROOF (Q&A)
AI asks you 3 specific questions about what you did. You answer in detail. AI reads your responses, checks for specificity and honesty, and scores your execution quality. The hardest to fake.
FREE · ALL TIERS
📸
PHOTO PROOF
Take a screenshot or photo of your completed work — the output, the screen, the result. AI reviews it against your task brief. Fast to submit, clear to verify. Works for most tasks.
RISE + APEX
🎤
VOICE PROOF
Record a voice note explaining what you did. AI transcribes it, analyses your explanation for depth and accuracy. The most natural way to report — and the hardest to bluff through.
