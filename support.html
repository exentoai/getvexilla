// Frequently Asked
What is Vexilla AI?
+
Vexilla AI is an AI-powered life execution system for ambitious individuals aged 15–22. It diagnoses your execution profile, builds a personalised 7-day Phase 0 plan, and holds you accountable through daily proof verification. No partial credit. Miss a day — streak resets.
How do I cancel my subscription?
+
On iOS: Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Vexilla AI → Cancel.

On Android: Go to Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & Subscriptions → Subscriptions → Vexilla AI → Cancel.

You will retain access until the end of your billing period.
I'm having trouble logging in. What do I do?
+
Try signing out and signing back in. If using Google Sign-In, ensure you have a stable internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, email us at ceo.vexilla.ai@gmail.com with your device model and OS version.
What is Phase 0 and what comes after?
+
Phase 0 is a 7-day foundation building period personalised to your goals and available hours. The AI builds your roadmap based on your execution diagnosis. Completing Phase 0 unlocks Phase 1 and beyond — a full progression system built around your specific domain, whether that's JEE, fitness, freelancing, coding, or anything else.
What data does Vexilla AI collect?
+
We collect your name, email, onboarding responses, and daily task proof submissions. This data is used solely to power your personalised execution system. We do not sell your data to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
I was charged but can't access Operator Mode.
+
Try logging out and logging back in — this refreshes your subscription status. If the issue persists within 24 hours of purchase, email us at ceo.vexilla.ai@gmail.com with your order ID and we'll resolve it immediately.
